1 江順楠 國立台灣大學 社會學系 博士後研究員 Historicizing Problematization — a Study of Coexisting Agrifood Solutions to Malnutrition in the Philippines

While the framing of grand challenges has shaped how global society perceives collective problems, STS scholars have developed the concepts of problematization and problem-solution coupling to analyze global issues such as climate change. However, the current STS theorization of problematization tends to center on examining a specific technology and its assumed problem. This research suggests a need to “rescale” technology analysis from centering on one specific technology to studying a collection of coexisting solutions. To achieve the goal, this research aims to re-theorize the concept of problematization by studying a collection of five distinct types of agrifood solutions to malnutrition in the Philippines: rice biofortification, rice fortification, school gardening, brown rice consumption, and moringa supplementation. This research further develops the concept of “problem-solution constellation” to demonstrate how the problem of malnutrition and agrifood solutions connect in each historical period. My discovery shows that the coexistence of agrifood solutions to malnutrition is not merely a contemporary phenomenon but has existed throughout history. This research further highlights how a set of agrifood approaches have kept re-emerging to address different formations of the problem of malnutrition in three historic periods. In the first period, discovering how vitamin B1 deficiency caused beriberi went hand-in-hand with exploring multiple agrifood solutions to deal with beriberi as a disease while some agrifood solutions competed for policy implementation. In the second period, the political rationale of the authoritarian regime dominated the formulation of dual problems — how to achieve food self-insufficiency and how to utilize nutrition governance to address protein deficiency — and the development of politically-motivated agrifood solutions with diverse ideologies. In the third period, the predominant focus on micronutrient deficiency led the government to launch a coordinated initiative containing various solutions focusing on the constitution of food. Juxtaposing the problem-solution constellation of these three historical periods also reveal certain agrifood solutions that did not emerge as a viable solution in a specific period, which I term “non-solutions.” Overall, the historical analysis of problem-solution constellation advances STS scholars’ theorization on problematization in two ways. It shows that there are coexisting solutions in multiple historical periods, underscoring the importance of foregrounding the coexistence of agrifood solutions. Based on the discovery, I suggest that it would be more generative for STS scholars to replace the current framing of problem-solution coupling with problem-solution constellation so that scholars could be more attentive to coexisting solutions. Furthermore, while most of the problematization analysis in the STS field does not focus on the historical aspect, this research shows that the historical analysis could reveal non-solutions that did not appear to be a policy solution in a specific period and shed light on the potential of transforming the contemporary problem-solution constellation. In summary, this research urges STS researchers to “rescale” from technology-centered analysis to the analysis of problem-solution constellation and suggests that this rescaling of analysis could illuminate how we approach global issues.

台灣政府在1970年代從美國無線電公司引進半導體製造技術，經常被視為是國家強力介入科技與經濟發展的經典範例，但是本文重新省視在引進半導體製造技術中扮演關鍵角色、以潘文淵為首的「美洲技術顧問團」（Technical Advisory Concil, TAC）早期協助台灣政府引進半導體技術的歷史，認為可以從跨國科技史（the transantional history of science and technology）的觀點來理解潘文淵與TAC的成員其中的定位與角色。過往對於像是半導體產業在東亞國家如台灣和南韓的成功，發展型國家理論認為是國家與科技官僚的強力介入所造成，但是經濟社會學者則認為發展型國家理論忽略東亞社會本身的能動性，而認為應該從在地社會本身的條件進行分析。然而，潘文淵與TAC的成員多屬外國公民、亦未曾在台灣定居，卻代表中華民國來行動且有明確的國族認同，使其具備「既不屬於國家、又代表國家」的特色，並且在行動中企圖將從美國跨境而來的半導體製造技術，透過技術選擇與建構，使其與台灣社會與在地產業進行「耦合」。因此，本文認為跨國科技史的觀點可以整合雙方觀點對於國家與社會之間的解釋優勢，解釋何以工研院與TAC能成功地將半導體跨國科技與台灣在地社會整合，為台灣半導體產業建立基礎。
3 黃獻緯 國立台灣大學 生物產業傳播暨發展學系 博士生 The Systematic Volumes: The Forest Management and the Controversy of Wasabi in Ali mountain, Chiayi, Taiwan

In the studies of forestry management, it is a common phenomenon that the state faces resistance from the original residents and agricultural workers in the process of forest governance. This study examines the importance of focusing on the vertical dimension through the controversies between wasabi planting and soil and water conservation in Ali mountain, Chiayi County, Taiwan. Through the landslides caused by Typhoon Morakot, this study analyzes the controversy, discourses, technologies, and laws, and points out two points. First, the Forestry Bureau's lack of attention to the vertical dimensions makes it difficult for forest guardians to work; the lack of awareness of unexpected events leads to the absence of early warning in legislation and law enforcement. Second, systematic attention to the vertical dimensions is necessary for forestry management and can also expand the theoretical implications of measuring geopolitics.
4 龔文翎 紐約州立大學奧爾巴尼分校
State University of New York, University at Albany		 社會學系 博士候選人 Conceiving “Maternal Genetic Responsibility”: Two-staged Assignment of Reproductive Responsibility in the Infertility Clinical Context

Previous studies have provided considerable insights into the assignment of reproductive responsibility and gender inequality. Females, compared to males, often bear a disproportionate burden of responsibility for any potential “faulty” conditions during reproduction, including infertility, miscarriage, or detected fetal abnormalities, etc. Yet, existing studies mostly present a one-dimensional assignment of reproductive responsibility, overlooking its intricate dynamics with clinical technology and scientific knowledge in clinicians’ daily practices. Consequently, there remains a paucity of comprehension regarding the role of this assignment process in driving the continued biomedicalization of reproduction.

Based on interviews with 43 fertility clinicians and non-participant observations in Taiwan between 2017 and 2019, this research argues that “the responsibility for infertility” during IVF (in-vitro fertilization) is divided into two stages. In the first stage, clinicians aim to equally emphasize maternal and paternal responsibility for “failing to get pregnant naturally.” However, in the second stage, the responsibility for “the failed IVF outcome” is once again unequally assigned to both genders as clinicians assume clinical technology ICSI (intracytoplasmic sperm injection) resolve the issue of male infertility and adopt an uneven knowledge infrastructure concerning the genetic impact of egg and sperm on embryonic development. The genetic impact of the female egg on the IVF outcome is exclusively emphasized, leading to an imbalanced focus on what I term “maternal genetic responsibility” and a lack of discussion on “paternal genetic responsibility,” which legitimates the increasing clinical intervention of female eggs, endorses the growing services geared towards “accumulating” and “nurturing” eggs, and encourages proactive management on the maternal body at the molecular level in the IVF market.

This research demonstrates to what extent clinical technology and scientific knowledge mediate the assignment of reproductive responsibility, through which the locus of inegalitarian responsibility attribution between females and males has shifted to the genetic level of human reproductive cells. The dialectical mediation subtly naturalizes a biologically determined sex/gender difference and perpetuates existing gendered divisions of reproductive labor along with the geneticization of maternal responsibility. This process further advances the biomedicalization of reproduction.

本文將以鐵道作為殖民現代性之尺度，追溯日本博物學者田中芳男(1838-1916)《台灣帖》(1909)所蒐羅之影像，並參照十九世紀中葉至二十世紀初日本所受到西方影響之攝影史脈絡。田中芳男為日本幕末、明治時期的博物學者、動物學者、植物學者、物產學者，除了被稱為「日本博物館之父 」並創建了東京國立博物館和上野動物園。1908年，作為貴族院的議員田中芳男受台灣總督府佐久間左馬太之邀，前來臺灣參與1908年10月24日臺灣鐵路縱貫線全通式典禮，自1908年10月18日搭乘軍艦「姉川艦」渡臺在基隆上岸，10月31日搭乘商輪櫻丸號離臺，除了乘坐櫻丸號商輪時收到的地圖與寫真帖，他也將臺灣鐵道旅行沿途所收集的印刷品與繪葉書影像集結成臺灣行旅記事《台灣帖》，堆疊出彼時來臺十二日間的食、衣、住、行、育、樂。本文分就《台灣帖》中諸多面向試析田中芳男之臺灣縱貫鐵道旅行與「殖民現代性」之關聯，以火車時刻表作為時間的尺度，以蒸汽火車在城市間的移動作為島嶼距離的尺度，以寫真帖跟繪葉書作為視覺再現的尺度，以建築場地平面圖與城市案內圖作為人在空間中的尺度，包括身體規訓、地景變遷、風土物產甚至休閒娛樂，這些影像與印刷文字除了與當時臺灣的攝影發展密切相關也象徵著背後的殖民支配。
